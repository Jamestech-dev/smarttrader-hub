Basics ‚Äî How crypto trading works

Crypto markets run 24/7. Volume, liquidity, and news drive price. Understand trend, momentum, and support/resistance before entering.

Technical Analysis

Combine candlesticks with indicators. Use confluence for higher probability trades.

  • Trend filter: use 50 EMA / 200 EMA
  • Entry: retest of broken resistance
  • Confirmation: RSI divergence or MACD cross

Risk Management

Position sizing = (account * risk%) / stop distance. Keep RR >= 1.5

  • Risk per trade: 0.5% ‚Äî 2%
  • Daily stop: if you lose X% stop trading
  • Smaller size on high-volatility coins
